# TinyAtom Atom Submissions

TinyAtom is a desktop app for building and using small, focused apps called atoms. Atoms submitted to the
public TinyAtom marketplace are collected in this repository for review.

## How to submit an atom

You do everything inside the TinyAtom desktop app. There is nothing to fork, clone, or edit here by hand.

1. Build your atom in TinyAtom until it works the way you want.
2. Open the atom and choose the option to publish it to the public marketplace.
3. TinyAtom packages your atom and submits it here for review automatically.
4. A maintainer reviews your submission.
5. Once it is accepted, your atom appears in the TinyAtom marketplace for anyone to install.

## Updating an atom

Raise your atom's version in TinyAtom and publish it again. The new version goes through the same review.
